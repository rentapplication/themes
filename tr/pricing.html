Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a recipient?
We count times when you add a recipient to a project. The same address added to two different projects counts as two times. The recipient limit is reset in the beginning of each billing period.
How is storage space calculated?
Storage space is a sum of all files uploaded across all your requests (including archived ones). The limit is not reset every month. To clean up storage space, you can delete individual recipients or entire requests.
What if I want to upgrade?
You can upgrade or downgrade anytime. The billing date remains the same, while the difference in the cost (proportional to the number of days) will be added to (or subtracted from) your next payment.
What happens if I exceed my limits?
Your existing reminders will be running safe! Clients will also be able to upload new files. You just won't be able to add new recipients.
Can I get a refund?
We stand by the quality of our product, so you can always count on a 100% refund of your last month. No questions asked.
Do you provide discounts?
We offer a 50% lifetime discount for students and non-profit organizations. Please get in touch for details.
