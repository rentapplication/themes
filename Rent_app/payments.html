How It Works

On any form you create, you can specify an "extra" fee in addition to Rent Application's base fee (base fee is calculated by the reports requested).

Extra fees can then be viewed, managed, and withdrawed to your account using this page

Withdrawing Funds

Withdrawals of accumulated fees is processed via ACH transfer to your checking account.

Withdrawals are subject to the following restrictions:

  • Minimum account balance of $100 to initiate a withdrawal
  • $4.95 fee per withdrawal initiated.
  • On closing an account, all funds will be paid to the account of record via ACH regardless of the previous withdrawal time and current balance.

Due to compliance reasons and to prevent money laundering, Rent Application requires the full name of the person applying and some additional verification during the application process.

Costs

Setup Fee

Rent Application charges a one-time $34.95 setup fee to configure your account to be able to accept your own application fees.

Processing Fee

For each application processed with your additional fee, a credit card processing fee of $0.30 + [ 2.9% of the total amount ] will be charged and taken from the amount received.

Withdrawing Fee

Rent Application charges a fee of $4.95 for each withdrawal request.
