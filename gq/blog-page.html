Meet Your New (Customer Happiness) Hero

June 23, 2017
|In Community, News
|By Chris Harris

What’s up everyone? As one of the people you’ll interact with while learning about insurance and what makes Quilt different, I felt you should know a little bit about the face in the corner of your chat box. My name is Chris Harris, and I just joined the Quilt team as your Customer Happiness Hero. (I wish I could take credit for my title.) That means it’s my job to have everyone shouting from the rooftops about Quilt’s amazing customer experience.

I come to Quilt with an extensive insurance background and will help you in any way I can, whether it’s answering questions about insurance, assisting you with our digital process, or explaining the options to help you make the right choice for your needs.

Having worked with more traditional agencies in the past, I‘ve seen firsthand what happens when an agent puts what’s good for themselves ahead of what’s good for their customers, and how that can hurt a family trying to do the right thing by getting coverage. Most agents have been privy to a nightmare scenario where a person loses almost everything because they were made to believe they had more protection than they really did.

Some of the things I saw left a bad taste in my mouth, and was one of the reasons I stepped away from insurance for a few years – so much so that even being in the same industry made me queasy. Fast forward to 2017, and lo and behold, a dream opportunity to do things the right way! One of the best parts about Quilt is that I’m not paid on commission (and neither is anyone else who’s here to help you), so there’s never a decision to make between doing what’s right for the customer and what’s right for a paycheck.

Quilt also allows me to take a more natural approach to talking about insurance. I’m here to make insurance simple, not to sell you anything. That means more people will understand exactly what it is they’re buying, all while completing the entire process online and without even a phone call in most cases. (Who has time for that?)

Integrity and honesty are the cornerstones to a successful business, and the fact that it’s part of the foundation here is why I’m excited to spread the Quilt gospel to anyone and everyone willing to listen. I look forward to speaking with all of you when you visit the site, and I hope you have a better summer than LeBron!
